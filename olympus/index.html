Olympus Medical

In an effort to increase potential patient screenings, the website ColonoscopyToday.com was launched as a part of a Direct to Patient marketing program. This direct to patient marking approach focuses on driving consumers to learn more about the importance of colorectal cancer screenings. This responsive website serves as a digital solution to reach the target age group for screenings which is 50 years and older. The website encourages patients to take action through a positive message that getting screened can save lives, while dispelling misconceptions about the preparation and screening process.
