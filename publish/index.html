P3 Publish

P3 Publish is a website content management system that was custom-built specifically for orthopedic, spine, and neuro medical practices. It was designed to make it easy for doctors and medical office personnel to log in and make content updates to their websites, as well as manage appointment requests and testimonials submitted by patients.

There are three different theme options associated with the P3 Publish content management system meant to highlight information for different practice sizes and specialties.

P3 Publish Themes

Katie provides ongoing support and maintenance for the themes associated with the P3 Publish CMS. The navigation structure and layout of the P3 Publish themes are based on research of patient behavior, with the goal of converting more website visitors into new patients. These themes rely heavily on the design and information architecture to effectively communicate with the user.
