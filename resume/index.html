About

With over a decade of experience, I specialize in designing and developing custom websites and applications, utilizing a wide range of technologies while delivering engaging interfaces that focus on user experience, accessibility, performance, and functionality.

I have an insatiable interest in creative problem-solving and love taking on new projects that present complex, measurable data that can be iterated on long after implementation. I work with cross-functional teams to distill complex requirements into thoughtful, thorough, and delightful products.

Over the years, I have come to truly enjoy the process of evaluating the scope of a product and providing new business ideas, innovations, and values that meet user needs as well as business objectives. Using an agile workflow, I typically guide clients from research and planning, to conceptualizing with prototypes, and finally to testing and implementation.

I am always eager to learn new skills, and am open to new opportunities and experiences. My days are spent with thoughtful, creative, and honest people and I strive to display all of those qualities as well.
