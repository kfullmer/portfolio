About

Katie Fullmer specializes in designing and developing custom web sites and applications, as well as online and print advertising. With over seven years of experience in web design and development, her broad expertise enables her to utilize a wider range of technologies while delivering custom interfaces that focus on user experience, performance, and functionality. She continually strives to gain experience by challenging herself to stay at the forefront of web technology and standards. She is driven, self-motivated and enjoys the creative problem solving process that web design and development offers.
