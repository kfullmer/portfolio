Meningococcal Infographic

Partnering with the Pfizer Global Meningococcal franchise, we created this infographic for a military audience to promote awareness and provide facts surrounding the threat of invasive meningococcal disease. Aligning with the Pfizer brand, this asset provides visual data surrounding the risk of meningococcal disease and how long term protection can be achieved with the use of Pfizer's ACWY meningococcal vaccine Nimenrix®.

Presenting the most compelling data at the top and drawing the viewer's eye down the page with various design elements, the content is organized into four sections: disease information, effectiveness data, manufacturing capacity, and environmental impact. Custom iconography and compelling stats keep the viewer engaged while providing clear and obvious takeaways in this effective infographic.
